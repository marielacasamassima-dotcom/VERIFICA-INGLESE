02 Reading Comprehension 10 pts

Read the text carefully, then answer the questions below in complete sentences.

A Surprising Week

Last week was full of surprises. On Monday afternoon I found a wallet in the park, on a bench near the fountain. It had money and cards inside, so I ran to the police station and gave it to the officer at the desk. She said I was very honest and wrote my name in a register.

On Tuesday my class went on a school trip to a science museum. My friend Anna thought it was absolutely amazing — she sent photos to all her friends and wrote a long post about it that evening.

On Wednesday I got some great news: I won a prize in a drawing competition. The teacher gave me a certificate in front of the whole class and everyone clapped.

On Thursday I made a chocolate cake for my mum's birthday. I followed a recipe online and it came out perfectly. She said it was the best cake she had ever eaten.

On Friday I left home very early because I didn't want to miss the first train. I knew the journey would take two hours and I had to be at my cousin's house by noon. It was a long but wonderful week.
