My Busy Week

Last week was really busy for me. On Monday morning I woke up late, so I ran to school and didn't eat breakfast. My friend Tom wrote me a message during the lesson – he told me he had bought a new guitar at the weekend.

On Tuesday we had a maths test. I studied a lot, but I didn't do very well because I forgot some formulas. In the afternoon my mum drove me to the sports centre. I swam for an hour and then we went home.

On Wednesday I met my classmates at the library. We read two chapters of our project book and spoke about our ideas. On Thursday something great happened: my uncle came to visit and brought a huge pizza. We ate together and watched a funny film.

By Friday I was tired, but I felt happy because I had finished all my homework and had sent it to my teacher.
